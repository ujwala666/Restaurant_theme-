About Us

Welcome to Amma Chethi Vanta Restaurant, one of the most loved family restaurants in the city. We started our journey in 2026 with a mission to provide delicious food, quality service, and a memorable dining experience for every customer.

Our restaurant is famous for Italian dishes, spicy Indian starters, grilled foods, desserts, and refreshing beverages. Every dish is prepared by experienced chefs using fresh vegetables, premium spices, and hygienic cooking methods.

our restaurant offers a beautiful dining area for families, friends, birthday parties, college events, and office celebrations. We also provide online food delivery and table reservation services.
